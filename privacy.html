Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to the PDF to PRACTICE. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Data Collection

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit ANY of your personal data or uploaded files.

This application is entirely client-side. When you upload a PDF (like a DPP or PYQ), the file is processed locally within your web browser on your device. We do not have servers that receive your documents, and we do not track your practice scores or history.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's LocalStorage and IndexedDB to save your theme preferences (Light/Dark mode) and your practice history (e.g., bookmarks, recent sessions, notes) so you can resume them later. This data remains on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party CDNs (like Cloudflare and jsDelivr) to serve essential libraries (like PDF.js and Tailwind CSS). These providers may collect basic connection information (like IP addresses) as part of their standard web serving logs.

4. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we don't collect your email address, we encourage you to review this page periodically.
